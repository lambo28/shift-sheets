Request Shift Swap

Select two drivers and the dates they each give up. Their shifts will be swapped. Validation is applied before saving.

Validation Rules
  • Both drivers must have a shift on their respective dates.
  • No overlapping shifts after the swap.
  • Minimum 8 hours rest between consecutive shifts.
  • Swap must not exceed each driver's weekly assignment limit.
