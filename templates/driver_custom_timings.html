How Custom Timings Work
  • Priority: Lower numbers = higher priority. Most specific match wins.
  • Assignment Scope: Rules can apply to specific assignments or all assignments for this driver.
  • Criteria: Empty criteria means "any". The more specific, the higher the effective priority.
  • Fallback: If no custom timing matches, default shift times are used.
